Not just events (but mostly events)

This site was started to scratch an itch. What kind of events are happening and what kind of spaces are available in Richmond for developers, designers, and the entrepreneurs who work side-by-side with them?

The content here is aggregated to provide a central view into what's going on. It's run by one-person and events are pulled from a hopefully ever-growing list of event hosts and groups.

If you have an event, event series, or group that hosts events which you'd like to suggest, please contact me via Twitter @RVAConnect.
